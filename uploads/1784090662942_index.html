Our Story

Founded in 2020, Brew & Bean started with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment.

Our journey began with a trip through the coffee regions of Colombia, Ethiopia, and Japan, where we learned about the art and science of coffee production.

Today, we continue to explore new flavors and techniques, bringing the world's best coffees to your cup.
